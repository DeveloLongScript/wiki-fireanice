# Classified Resources
Classified Resources are resources held under the staff resources that cannot be shared without getting punished for leaking in the rules.

## Punishable under
Leaking, is a rule for staff punishable under getting instantly **suspended and permenantly blacklisted**.

## Why does the wiki avoid classified resources
FireAnIce Wiki, trys to avoid classified resources to make sure the whole wiki can be read by anybody, staff or not. If you have a problem with a resource on FireAnIce (being classified), please go to the [Cease Form](/cease).
