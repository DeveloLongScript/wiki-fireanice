# Stone Mine
The stone mine is the second of the 8 mines in the Overworld.

## Trades
### Big Shopkeeper
#### Pickaxes
 - 16 Cobble + Wood Pickaxe -> Stone Pickaxe
 - 32 Cobble + Stone Pickaxe -> Tier 2 Stone Pickaxe (Efficiency I)
 - 48 Cobble + Tier 2 Stone Pickaxe -> Tier 3 Stone Pickaxe (Efficiency II)
 - 64 Cobble + Tier 3 Stone Pickaxe -> Tier 4 Stone Pickaxe (Efficiency III)

#### Armor
 - 1 Compressed Stone + Leather Helmet -> Stone Helmet
 - 1 Compressed Stone + Leather Chestplate -> Stone Chestplate
 - 1 Compressed Stone + Leather Leggings -> Stone Leggings
 - 1 Compressed Stone + Leather Boots -> Stone Boots
 - 2 Compressed Stone + Leather Helmet -> Tier 2 Stone Helmet (Protection I)
 - 2 Compressed Stone + Leather Chestplate -> Tier 2 Stone Chestplate (Protection I)
 - 2 Compressed Stone + Leather Leggings -> Tier 2 Stone Leggings (Protection I)
 - 2 Compressed Stone + Leather Boots -> Tier 2 Stone Boots (Protection I)

#### Shards
 - 64 Compressed Stone -> Stone Shard

### Compression
 - 64 Cobblestone -> 1 Compressed Stone
 - 64 Cobblestone + 64 Cobblestone -> 2 Compressed Stone
 - 1 Compressed Stone -> 64 Cobblestone
 - 1 Smooth Stone -> 9 Cobblestone
